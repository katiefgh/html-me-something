The Story So Far...

In the beginning, there were four: a cleric, devoted to Malfus, a rogue no one could trust, a ranger with impeccable aim, and a fighter with a tireless spirit.

After an encounter with orcs, only one survived; the cleric received the heads of his fallen friends on pikes, a gruesome reminder and warning to travel wisely. He soon found new friends to protect.

The barbarian, cleric, druid, fighter, rogue, and wizard traveled for many months in relative harmony before encountering a half-elf warlock. Although they remained suspicious of her motives, they took her into the group, only to find she had wandered off in the night before long.
